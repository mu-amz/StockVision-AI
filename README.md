# 📈 StockVision AI

### Machine Learning Powered Stock Risk Analysis & Forecasting Platform

<p align="center">
<b>Analyze • Predict • Invest • Grow</b>
</p>

StockVision AI is an intelligent stock market analysis platform that combines Machine Learning, Real-Time Market Data, Predictive Analytics, and Expert Insights to help investors make informed decisions.

The platform enables companies to manage stock information, experts to analyze market trends and provide investment guidance, and users to monitor stock performance, view forecasts, and explore market opportunities through a Flutter mobile application.

---

# 🚀 Key Features

## 🤖 Machine Learning Based Stock Forecasting

The platform uses Regression Algorithms to analyze historical stock performance and predict future stock prices.

Features:

* Historical trend analysis
* Next-day stock prediction
* Stock growth forecasting
* Market trend identification
* Risk assessment support

The prediction engine continuously learns from historical stock data to generate future price estimates.

---

## 📊 Real-Time Stock Market Analysis

Experts can access real-time stock market information through integrated market data services.

Capabilities:

* Live stock prices
* Market trend monitoring
* Historical stock analysis
* Company performance tracking
* Investment opportunity identification

The platform fetches and processes market data for intelligent analysis and forecasting.

---

## 📈 Predictive Analytics Engine

Using Machine Learning Regression Models, the system:

* Analyzes stock history
* Detects market patterns
* Calculates future stock trends
* Predicts next-day stock values
* Determines upward and downward movements

Prediction outputs include:

* Predicted Price
* Current Price
* Trend Direction
* Historical Performance

---

## 🏢 Company Stock Management

Registered companies can manage their stock information through the platform.

Features:

* Add stocks
* Update stock prices
* Daily stock performance tracking
* Historical stock maintenance
* Company profile management

Daily updates help improve prediction accuracy and market analysis.

---

## 👨‍💼 Expert Analysis Module

Financial experts can:

* Monitor stock market activity
* Analyze company performance
* View stock trends
* Generate forecasts
* Share investment tips
* Respond to investor queries

This provides users with both AI-driven and expert-driven insights.

Capabilities:
